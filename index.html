Our Story

A cozy escape from the city bustle

Nestled behind Hiranandani Meadows, Amigos Cafe is more than a restaurant — it's a two-level sanctuary where time slows down. Whether you're settling in for a quiet work session, a candlelit date, or a lively catch-up with friends, there's a corner here with your name on it.

We're known for our legendary hot chocolate, handcrafted pastas, and a menu that fuses Italian classics with Mexican boldness. Every dish is made with obsessive care and a whole lot of heart.
